Admission Process

From application to enrollment — here’s how to get started.

1

Inquiry

Reach out to our admissions office to request information and schedule a tour.

2

Application

Purchase application form available at the school office, Complete the form and submit required documents.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to complete an entrance exam or interview.

4

Decision

Admissions decisions are communicated via email within two weeks of application completion.

5

Enrollment

Accepted students will receive enrollment instructions and deadlines.

6

Orientation

New students and families are invited to attend orientation sessions before the school year begins.
